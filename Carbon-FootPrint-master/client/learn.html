Your Carbon Footprint

PocketFutures is a mobile device app designed to promote individual responsibility toward environmental sustainability. By tracking the distance traveled at various speeds, PocketFutures can determine the amount traveled by vehicle and the corresponding cost in fossil fuels. By combining contextual data retrieved from the mobile device with information volunteered by the user, the app can also estimate environmental costs resulting from utilities and food consumption. Finally, PocketFutures provides a simple method for the user to contribute to the well-being of the ecosystem by providing a quick and simple method to donate to environmental causes.
